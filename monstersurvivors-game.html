About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an intense survival game that puts your skills to the ultimate test. As a lone survivor in a world overrun by monsters, you must use your wits, weapons, and special abilities to stay alive.

With each wave of enemies, the challenge grows more intense, forcing you to adapt your strategy and upgrade your character to survive.
